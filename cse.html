COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) was established in the year 1997.
The Department offers qualitative training and scope for more research.
The Dept. provides state-of- art computing facilities to the students.
It also promotes active industry-institute collaboration by identifying areas of undergraduate and
postgraduate degree students.The Courses offered in the department meet the requirements of both industry and research.

The curriculum is designed to train the students in developing application programs (Software) and also designing and developing computing systems (Hardware).

Our Department is supported by disciplined and dedicated faculty. They always come forward to enhance the Excellency of the department academically.
We own a crowning glory in the list of its achievements. Backed with this academic excellence, more than 70% of our students are being placed in different
reputed MNC’s every year, setting bench marks in the campus recruitments.
